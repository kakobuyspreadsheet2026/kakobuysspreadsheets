How to use the Kakobuy Spreadsheet

The kakobuy spreadsheet loop is simple: pull rows here, push orders through Kakobuy. Keep these steps in mind every haul:

① Browse or Search Products

Use search and category filters in the hub to find what you want. The database includes 100,000+ products across sneakers, clothing, bags, electronics, and more—sourced from Taobao, 1688, and Weidian.

② Compare Products & Find the Best Batch

Before you commit, compare multiple listings:

  • Check different sellers offering the same item
  • Compare pricing and QC photos
  • Look for consistent quality across batches

That way you pick a better batch instead of guessing.

③ Check QC Photos and Product Details

Open a product to review price (often with USD context), seller name, and QC photos. QC shows what buyers actually received—use it before you order.

④ Copy the Product Link

Each row includes a link you can copy into Kakobuy. Fix truncated URLs if needed.

⑤ Place Your Order via Kakobuy

Paste the link into Kakobuy and complete your order. Your agent handles seller communication, inspection, warehouse storage, and international shipping options.

⑥ Review QC & Confirm Shipment

After the item reaches the warehouse, review updated QC, confirm it meets your expectations, and request exchange or return within the allowed window. Only ship what you are happy with.

⑦ Track & Receive Your Package

Once shipped, tracking comes from your agent. Delivery often takes 7–20 days depending on the line you choose.
