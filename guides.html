Eight stops from row to parcel

1 From spreadsheet row to Kakobuy order

A kakobuy spreadsheet row is only a curated hint: title, price context, maybe a QC thumbnail. The buy path starts when you copy the product URL into Kakobuy’s item field and submit—Kakobuy negotiates the domestic leg, not this website.

  • Fix truncated links before you pay; broken rows should be skipped or replaced in-hub.
  • If two rows look like the same batch, compare notes, then pick one link—don’t double-order by accident.
  • Domestic shipping to Kakobuy’s warehouse is part of their flow, not something this marketing site tracks.

2 Wallet top-ups and when your balance moves

Kakobuy runs on a prepaid-style wallet for most purchases. Top up before you chase limited stock, and keep a buffer for domestic freight surprises.

  • Use whichever funding methods Kakobuy lists for your region—cards, wallets, etc.
  • Refunds and withdrawals go through Kakobuy’s own forms, never through this domain.
  • Failed payments usually mean limits, bank checks, or verification—retry inside Kakobuy, not here.

3 Rehearsal, volumetric weight, and surprise postage

Spreadsheet cells may show a guess at international postage. Kakobuy’s rehearsal step repacks your items, measures real weight, and often applies volumetric rules—final cost can jump or drop versus the estimate.

  • Run rehearsal when boxes are bulky or you combine multiple pieces.
  • Overpaid estimates usually return to wallet credit; underpaid means you top up again.
  • Read Kakobuy’s line-specific notes—some carriers price by volume, not scale weight alone.

Tip: treat any number in the sheet as planning only; treat Kakobuy’s rehearsal screen as authoritative before you click “pay shipping.”

4 Warehouse QC beats seller photos in the sheet

Rows may include seller QC or community screenshots. Kakobuy’s warehouse QC set is what you approve or reject before international postage—lighting, angles, and defects should be judged there.

  • Reject or request exchange while Kakobuy still holds the goods.
  • After you green-light outbound shipping, remedies narrow—be slow and picky upstream.
  • For deeper QC reading, see our QC guide.

5 Storage, combining items, choosing a line

Free storage is time-boxed—check Kakobuy’s current clock. Combine parcels only after each piece passes QC so you are not shipping a dud to save a few dollars.

  • Pick shipping lines for your risk tolerance: budget lines vs branded carriers vs insured options.
  • Declare values truthfully if Kakobuy asks—guessing wrong can create customs pain later.
  • Need weight nuance only? Read shipping weight & estimates.

6 Tracking silence, customs, and insurance

International scans pause all the time—hubs, handoffs, or customs review. That does not automatically mean a lost parcel; it means patience plus a Kakobuy ticket if you blow past the line’s stated window.

  • Optional insurance is worth it when the haul is heavy or high value—read exclusions.
  • Customs may inspect without updating public tracking; your local rules decide duties.
  • Damage claims need photo proof the moment the box arrives—Kakobuy sets the clock.

7 Refunds, tickets, and what we cannot touch

Our team curates spreadsheet rows and education. We do not access your Kakobuy wallet, cannot force refunds, and cannot speed up warehouse tickets.

  • Discord is for community Q&A; sensitive account issues belong to Kakobuy support.
  • Stale links? See dead links before you blame the agent.
  • Promotions, coupons, referrals—only Kakobuy decides eligibility.

8 Restricted goods: ask Kakobuy before you ship

Batteries, liquids, magnets, replicas with IP risk—many categories look fine in a marketplace screenshot but fail export or import rules. If a row looks edgy, pause and ask Kakobuy support before you pay international postage.

  • Cross-check Kakobuy’s prohibited list and your country’s import law.
  • Some carriers reject categories others allow—line choice matters as much as the item.
  • When in doubt, skip the buy; spreadsheets refresh faster than customs disputes.

This short guide exists so kakobuy spreadsheet users know what happens after they copy a link—everything chargeable still happens between you and Kakobuy. For exhaustive policy tables, rely on Kakobuy’s official help center and live account screens; we keep this page narrative, not encyclopedic.
